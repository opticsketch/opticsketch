Optic Sketch is an easy-to-use and affordable 3D optical simulation tool that can help you assess component performance, and quickly layout and simulate optical systems.

Optic Sketch is currrently available for Windows, and is local-first and subscription-free, we don't collect or store your data.

Current Features (Beta):

  • Scalar and polarization ray tracing
  • Interactive placement and alignment of sources and components
  • 3D view with easy navigation
  • Multi-field chromatic spot diagrams
  • Multi-threaded simulation
  • Incoherent and coherent detector diagrams
  • More analyses coming soon

Component types

  • Multiple source types
  • Singlets, aspheres and doublets
  • Spherical and aspherical mirrors
  • Transmission and reflection gratings
  • Beam splitters
  • Filters and dichroic mirrors
  • More source and component types, stock components and coatings coming soon
